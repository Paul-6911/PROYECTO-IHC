Medicamp - Responsive Bootstrap Template

Medicamp is a simple minimalistic, professional, responsive and interesting HTML template. It is suitable for medical and health related businesses or any corporate websites in this field. It has been developed using the latest technology and can be customized with rapid ease. It can also be befitted for medical centers, dentists, doctors, general practices, health cares or a hospital. The design of Medicamp is elegant, clean and professionally crafted - all of the elements are grouped and named in such a way that it is not burdensome customize everything

Contact Form

Contact form will use php to send emails. So just open sendmail.php file. Change the current email templatesell@gmail.com to your email address. Your form is ready.

* Contact form require web server to send email. Emails can go to your spam/junk folder.
* If you use custom email like yourname@yourcompany.com then may be your mail client can block php mail completely. I recommend to use gmail or athor popular email address. 
* If you want perfect php mail system. please use https://github.com/PHPMailer/PHPMailer
 
 

Subscription Form

Default: Mailchimp

Current active subscribe form will save emails to mail chimp list. 

 

How to connect mail chimp list? 

Open custom.js and find following script. Just replace that mailchimp form action url with your mailchimp list url

 

How to find mailchimp form action URL?

Just go to your mailchimp account. Then create a new list or go to your existing list. Then Signup forms. Select  Embedded forms. You will find form action URL in this page.
